Who We Are

Gazelle Global is a specialist multinational recruitment and consulting firm helping organisations build world-class technology teams. Headquartered in the UK with offices across the Netherlands, Poland, Germany, and a growing US presence, we deliver talent and consulting solutions at scale across AI, data, cloud, and emerging technology programmes.

This document outlines our capabilities across Artificial Intelligence and Augmented Reality — both as an internal practitioner leveraging these technologies to sharpen our own delivery, and as a trusted partner helping clients build and scale their AI and AR functions globally.
